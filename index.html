How to Play

      
    
        
  • Pick a mode: Daily (one shared puzzle a day from the Top 200) or Free Play — where Normal uses the Top 200 and Hard mode opens up every team.
    • 
        
  • Guess any team number — its REBUILT robot attributes are compared to the target.
    • 
        
  • Green = exact match. Red = no match.
    • 
        
  • On Team # and Rookie Year, an arrow shows whether the target's value is 🔼 higher / 🔽 lower than your guess.
    • 
        
  • You have 5 guesses — turn the whole row green before you run out, or it's a loss.
    • 
      

      
The attribute columns

      

        
Clearance: Trench · Bump

        
Serialization: Rollerfloor · Dyerotor · Spindexer

        
Shooter: Turret · Double Turret · Dumper (single vs double turret = orange partial)

        
Climb: Climb · No Climb

        
Team Color: Red · Blue · Yellow · Green · Purple · Orange · Pink · Tan · White · Black · No major color (1–2 per robot — green = same set, yellow = shares one, gray = none)

        
Intake: Over Bumper Intake · Cut Bumper Intake
